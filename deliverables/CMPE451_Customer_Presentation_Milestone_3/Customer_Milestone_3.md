<summary><strong>1.1.1 Authentication</strong></summary>



* **1.1.1.1 Sign Up** 
  * **1.1.1.1.1** *Users shall provide their first name and last name.* ➡️ **Status: Not Started***
  * **1.1.1.1.2** *Users shall provide a username and e-mail address that are unique in the system.* ➡️ **Status: Completed***
  * **1.1.1.1.3** *Users shall be prevented from registration if they enter a username or email address that is already in use .* ➡️ **Status: Completed***
  * **1.1.1.1.4** *Users shall set a password that is longer than 8 characters and contains at least one upper-case letter, one lower-case letter, and one number.* ➡️ **Status: Not Started*** 
  * **1.1.1.1.5** *Users shall confirm the password by writing the same password again.* ➡️ **Status: Completed*** 
  * **1.1.1.1.6** *Users shall verify their registration process via email verification.* ➡️ **Status: Not Started***
  * **1.1.1.1.7** *Health professionals shall be able to register as privileged users.* ➡️ **Status: In Progress***
  * **1.1.1.1.8** *Health professionals shall provide all the information that users provide for registration.* ➡️ **Status: In Progress***
  * **1.1.1.1.9** *Health professionals shall provide some additional information regarding their educational backgrounds and professional experience.* ➡️ **Status: In progress***
  * **1.1.1.1.10** *Health professionals shall be verified by administrators.* ➡️ **Status: Not Started***
  * **1.1.1.1.11** *Health professionals shall be privileged after their proficiency is verified.* ➡️ **Status: Not Started***


* **1.1.1.2. Sign In**
  * **1.1.1.2.1** *Users shall provide their e-mails and passwords for signing in. ➡️ **Status: Completed***
  * **1.1.1.2.2** *Users shall get a warning message if the provided e-mail or password is wrong. ➡️ **Status: Completed***
  * **1.1.1.2.3** *Users shall see the home page after signing in successfully.➡️ **Status: Completed***
  * **1.1.1.2.4** *Users shall be able to reset their passwords.➡️ **Status: Completed***

* **1.1.1.3. Reset Password**
  * **1.1.1.3.1** *Users shall provide their e-mail accounts for resetting their passwords.➡️ **Status: Completed***
  * **1.1.1.3.2** *Users shall be able to select a new password after verifying the code that is sent to their emails.➡️ **Status: Completed***

<summary><strong>1.1.2. User Interactions</strong></summary>

* **1.1.2.1 Forum**
  * **1.1.2.1.1** *Users shall be able to create posts.➡️ **Status: Completed***
  * **1.1.2.1.2** *Users shall be able to comment under other users' posts and comments.➡️ **Status: Completed***
  * **1.1.2.1.3** *Users shall add a title while creating posts.➡️ **Status: Completed***
  * **1.1.2.1.4** *Users shall add body text to their posts and comments.➡️ **Status: Completed***
  * **1.1.2.1.5** *Users shall be able to add images to their posts and comments.➡️ **Status: Completed***
  * **1.1.2.1.6** *Users shall select categories of their posts from a specified set of categories.➡️ **Status: Completed***
  * **1.1.2.1.7** *Users shall be able to share locations in their posts and comments.➡️ **Status: Completed***
  * **1.1.2.1.8** *Users shall be able to read a post shared by another user.➡️ **Status: Completed***
  * **1.1.2.1.9** *Users shall be able to edit their posts and comments.➡️ **Status: Completed***
  * **1.1.2.1.10** *Users shall be able to delete their posts and comments.➡️ **Status: Completed***
  * **1.1.2.1.11** *Users shall be able to report other users' posts and comments.➡️ **Status: Not Started***
  * **1.1.2.1.12** *Users shall be able to upvote other users' posts and comments.➡️ **Status: Completed***
  * **1.1.2.1.13** *Users shall be able to see all posts.➡️ **Status: Completed***
  * **1.1.2.1.14** *Users shall be able to refer to an external link in their posts or comments using hyperlinks.➡️ **Status: Not Started***
  * **1.1.2.1.15** *Users shall get a notification after their posts or comments record an activity.➡️ **Status: Not Started***
  * **1.1.2.1.16** *Users shall see health professionals' comments on posts as top comments. ➡️ **Status: In Progress***
  * **1.1.2.1.17** *Users shall be able to follow health professionals.➡️ **Status: Not Started***
  * **1.1.2.1.18** *Users shall be able to unfollow health professionals.➡️ **Status: Not Started***
  * **1.1.2.1.19** *Users shall be able to follow categories.➡️ **Status: Not Started***
  * **1.1.2.1.20** *Users shall be able to unfollow categories.➡️ **Status: Not Started***
  * **1.1.2.1.21** *Health professionals shall be able to change the categories of any post.➡️ **Status: In Progress***
  * **1.1.2.1.22** *Admins shall get a notification after a post is reported. ➡️ **Status: Not Started***
  * **1.1.2.1.23** *Admins shall be able to delete any post or comment if required.➡️ **Status: Not Started***
  * **1.1.2.1.24** *Admins shall send a message to the post owner when a post is deleted.➡️ **Status: Not Started***
  

* **1.1.2.2 Profile Pages**
  
  * **1.1.2.2.1** *Users shall have a profile page.➡️ **Status: In Progress***
  * **1.1.2.2.2** *Users shall see other users' user type from their profile pages.➡️ **Status: Not Started***
  * **1.1.2.2.3** *Users shall see other users' avatars from their profile pages. ➡️ **Status: In Progress***
  * **1.1.2.2.4** *Users shall see other users' past comments and post from their profile pages.➡️ **Status: Not Started***
  * **1.1.2.2.5** *Users shall have their usernames visible on their profile pages.➡️ **Status: Completed***
  * **1.1.2.2.6** *Users shall have a label above their avatars that indicates their user type.➡️ **Status: In Progress***
  * **1.1.2.2.7** *Health professionals shall have a label above their avatars that indicates their user type.➡️ **Status: In Progress***
  * **1.1.2.2.8** *Health professionals shall have their real names visible on their profile pages.➡️ **Status: Not Started***
  * **1.1.2.2.9** *Admins shall have a label above their avatars that indicates they are admins.➡️ **Status: Not Started***

* **1.1.2.3 Chatting**

  * **1.1.2.2.1** *Users shall be able to send text messages to each other.➡️ **Status: Completed***
  * **1.1.2.2.2** *Users shall be able to block another user.➡️ **Status: Not Started***
  * **1.1.2.2.3** *Users shall be able to unblock a user that they blocked.➡️ **Status: Not Started***
  * **1.1.2.2.4** *Users shall not be able to send messages to users who blocked them.➡️ **Status: Not Started***
  * **1.1.2.2.5** *Users shall not be able to receive messages from users who blocked them.➡️ **Status: Not Started***

* **1.1.2.4 Timeline**

   * **1.1.2.4.1** *Users shall be able to see followed health professionals.➡️ **Status: Not Started***
   * **1.1.2.4.2** *Users shall be able to see followed categories.➡️ **Status: In Progress***
   * **1.1.2.4.3** *Users shall be able to see posts from followed health professionals with filtering and sorting options.➡️ **Status: Not Started***
   * **1.1.2.4.4** *Users shall be able to see posts from followed categories with filtering and sorting options.➡️ **Status: Not Started***

### 1.2 System Requirements

<ul>

<details>
<summary><strong>1.2.1. Search</strong></summary>

* **1.2.1.1.** *The system shall enable users to search for a health professional.➡️ **Status: In Progress***
* **1.2.1.2.** *The system shall enable users to search a tag. ➡️ **Status: In Progress***
* **1.2.1.3.** *The system shall enable users to make an advanced search based on a keyword and/or date and/or location.➡️ **Status: In Progress***
* **1.2.1.4.** *The system shall provide a sorting option that reorders posts by their posted date.➡️ **Status: Not Started***
* **1.2.1.5.** *The system shall provide a sort option that reorders posts by their number of comments.➡️ **Status: Not Started***
* **1.2.1.6.** *The system shall provide a sort option that reorders posts by their number of upvotes.➡️ **Status: Not Started***
* **1.2.1.7.** *The system shall provide a filtering option that filters posts according to their categories.➡️ **Status: Not Started***
* **1.2.1.8.** *The system shall provide a filtering option that filters posts according to their posted date.➡️ **Status: Not Started***
* **1.2.1.9.** *The system shall provide a filtering option that filters posts according to their owners' user type.➡️ **Status: Not Started***


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>1.2.2. Recommendation</strong></summary>

* **1.2.2.1.** *The system shall give recommendations to health professionals:*
  * **1.2.2.1.1** *Recommended posts shall be relevant to the proficiency of the health professional.➡️ **Status: Not Started***
  * **1.2.2.1.2.** *Recommended posts shall be relevant to recently replied post tags by the health professional.➡️ **Status: Not Started***

* **1.2.2.2.** *The system shall give recommendations to users:*
  * **1.2.2.2.1.** *Recommended posts shall be relevant to recently viewed and created post categories by the user.➡️ **Status: Not Started***
  * **1.2.2.2.2.** *The system shall recommend posts from followed health professionals.➡️ **Status: Not Started***
  * **1.2.2.2.3.** *The system shall recommend top posts from the categories.➡️ **Status: Not Started***
</details>


<details>
<summary><strong>1.2.3. Chatbot</strong></summary>

* **1.2.3.1.** *Chatbot shall provide a set of predetermined questions that users can select between them➡️ **Status: Not Started***.

* **1.2.3.2.** *Chatbot shall have a set of follow-up questions for every predetermined question➡️ **Status: Not Started***.

* **1.2.3.3.** *Chatbot shall ask follow-up predetermined questions after users select a question➡️ **Status: Not Started***.

* **1.2.3.4.** *Chatbot shall include predetermined questions about how users can use a specific functionality of the application.➡️ **Status: Not Started***

* **1.2.3.5.** *Chatbot shall provide information related to questions selected by the user.➡️ **Status: Not Started***

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>1.2.4. Notification</strong></summary>

* **1.2.4.1.** *The system shall give users and health professionals the option to enable/disable the notification system for themselves.➡️ **Status: Not Started***

* **1.2.4.2.** *The system shall send a notification to a user when a new post is created with followed tags.➡️ **Status: Not Started***

* **1.2.4.3.** *The system shall have notification settings about created, replied to, and followed posts.➡️ **Status: Not Started***
  * **1.2.4.3.1.** *Notification settings shall include the options 'notify when a health professional replied' and 'notify when any user replied.➡️ **Status: Not Started***
  * **1.2.4.3.2.** *Notification settings shall have an option to stop getting notifications from a post.➡️ **Status: Not Started***

* **1.2.4.4.** *The system shall send a notification to the admin when a user reports an inappropriate post.➡️ **Status: Not Started***

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>1.2.5. Forum</strong></summary>

* **1.2.5.1.** *The system shall show the number of upvotes and downvotes given to a post or comment.➡️ **Status: Completed***

* **1.2.5.2.** *The system shall show the number of comments made to a post or comment.➡️ **Status: In Progress***

* **1.2.5.3.** *The system shall show the category of a post.➡️ **Status: Completed***

* **1.2.5.4.** *The system shall recommend posts from followed health professionals and categories.➡️ **Status: Not Started***

* **1.2.5.5.** *The system shall delete the whole discussion thread if the main post is deleted.➡️ **Status: Completed***

* **1.2.5.6.** *The system shall highlight health professionals' comments on posts and display them as top comments.➡️ **Status: Completed***

* **1.2.5.7.** *The system shall provide healthcare-related categories to users.➡️ **Status: Completed***

* **1.2.5.8.** *The system shall display the top posts of the current category.➡️ **Status: In Progress***

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>1.2.6. Admin Features</strong></summary>

* **1.2.6.1.** *Admins shall be able to see reported posts and comments.➡️ **Status: Not Started***

* **1.2.6.2.** *Admins shall be able to ban users from the system temporarily or permanently.➡️ **Status: Not Started***

* **1.2.6.3.** *Admins shall be able to delete posts and comments.➡️ **Status: Not Started***

## 2.Non-Functional Requirements

<ul>

<details>
<summary><strong>2.1 Reliability Requirements </strong></summary>

* **2.1.1.** *The system shall always run up to 2000 users.➡️ **Status: In Progress***
* **2.1.2.** *The system shall be portable for website and application.➡️ **Status: Completed***
* **2.1.3.** *The system shall respond to any user no later than 4 seconds.➡️ **Status: Completed***


</details>
<details>
<summary><strong>2.2 Availability Requirements </strong></summary>

* **2.2.1.** *The system language shall be English.➡️ **Status: Completed***
* **2.2.2.** *The system shall be available as a native web application in browsers.➡️ **Status: Completed***
* **2.2.3.** *The system shall be available as a native mobile application on Android platforms.➡️ **Status: Completed***


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>2.3 Security </strong></summary>

* **2.3.1.** *The system shall prevent various cyber-attacks by providing SSL certificates to its users (HTTPS).➡️ **Status: In Progress***
* **2.3.2.** *The system shall perform input validation properly wherever input is received such as forms, chatbot, etc.➡️ **Status: In Progress***
* **2.3.3.** *The system shall provide an access control policy such that users cannot act outside of their intended permission.➡️ **Status: In Progress***
* **2.3.4.** *The system shall use two-factor authentication for users trying to log into their accounts.➡️ **Status: Not Started***
* **2.3.5.** *The system shall properly implement security configurations and perform any necessary hardening.➡️ **Status: In Progress***
* **2.3.6.** *The system shall ensure an ongoing plan for monitoring, triaging, and applying updates or configuration changes for the lifetime of the application➡️ **Status: In Progress**.
* **2.3.7.** *The system shall use the HTTPS protocol to transfer encrypted data over the web.➡️ **Status: Completed for the current version***

</details>


<details>
<summary><strong>2.4 Standards</strong></summary>

* **2.4.1.** *The annotations shall be compliant with the [W3C Web Annotation Data Model.](https://www.w3.org/TR/annotation-model/)➡️ **Status: In Progress***




</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>2.5. Legal Requirements </strong></summary>

* **2.5.1** *The system shall contain a comprehensive, clear, and accessible privacy policy.*➡️ **Status: Not Started***
* **2.5.2.** *The system shall comply with the Law on the Protection of Personal Data No. 6698 (KVKK).*➡️ **Status: Not Started***
* **2.5.3.** *The system shall comply with [GDPR](https://gdpr-info.eu/).* ➡️ **Status: Not Started***
* **2.5.4.** *Users shall accept the privacy policy suggested when registering.*➡️ **Status: Not Started***




</details>

</ul>
